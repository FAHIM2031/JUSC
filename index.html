Welcome to JUSC

Jahangirnagar University Science Club (JUSC) is a pioneering student-run organization founded with a vision to foster scientific knowledge, critical thinking, and innovation across campus and beyond. Established on August 17, 2005, JUSC is the first and most prominent science-based club at Jahangirnagar University, built on the principles of curiosity, creativity, and community service.


One of our landmark initiatives, the Jahangirnagar Science Olympiad, has grown into a national platform that encourages young talents to showcase their knowledge and compete with enthusiasm. It not only tests academic excellence but also creates a space for critical dialogue, innovation, and networking among budding scientists.
